3D & CGI Workflows

Whether building entire virtual sets from scratch or generating hyper-realistic product renders, my 3D pipeline is fully optimized for speed and fidelity. Every tool in this stack has been chosen for its specific role — together they cover concept through delivery without compromise.

  • Cinema 4D: Primary modeling, MoGraph effector-based animation, and procedural rigging. Used daily for broadcast-ready title sequences and abstract 3D motion.
  • Blender: Hard-surface and organic modeling, fluid & smoke simulations, geometry node-driven procedural environments, and dynamic particle systems.
  • Unreal Engine: Real-time rendering via Lumen and Nanite, virtual production LED wall workflows, and interactive archviz with cinematic-quality lighting.
  • Redshift: Biased GPU rendering for lightning-fast turnaround on complex scenes. Handles multi-layer EXR passes, volumetric fog, caustics, and subsurface scattering.
  • OctaneRender: Spectral lighting and atmospheric effects — physically accurate sky models, deep volumetric clouds, and HDRI-matched environment lighting for product photography-grade realism.

Compositing & Finishing

The final 10% that separates a good shot from a cinematic masterpiece.

The Foundry Nuke

Industry-standard node-based compositor for feature film and broadcast VFX. Handles complex green screen extraction using the Primatte and IBK keyers, multi-pass EXR recombination from 3D renders, and advanced camera tracking integration with SynthEyes data. The node graph allows unlimited non-destructive layering — essential when a single composite has dozens of 3D passes, generated elements, and live-action plates all working together simultaneously.

DaVinci Resolve

The world's most capable professional color grading and finishing platform. Used for primary node-based color correction (lift/gamma/gain), secondary qualifications to isolate skin tones and environmental elements, and film-print emulation using custom PowerGrades. Handles full pipeline conforming — bringing an offline Premiere cut into Resolve for final grade and delivery in both broadcast Rec.709 and HDR Dolby Vision masters with precise luma mapping and legal level output.

Mocha Pro

The gold standard for planar motion tracking and masking in professional post-production. Mocha Pro's planar tracker outperforms point-based trackers on difficult shots — screen replacements on reflective surfaces, roto-masking of fast-moving subjects, and lens distortion removal using its PowerMesh warp. Integrates seamlessly as an After Effects and Premiere plugin, and exports track data natively to Nuke and Flame. Essential for any shot requiring object removal, stabilization of handheld footage, or invisible VFX work.

Power Plugins & Scripts

The extended arsenal — third-party tools that push the limits of what is possible inside the host applications.

Video Copilot Element 3D

Real-time 3D object rendering directly inside After Effects. Used for extruded logo animations, 3D text fly-throughs, and GPU-accelerated particle-based 3D scenes without a full 3D app round-trip.

Red Giant Magic Bullet Suite

Premiere-grade color tools accessible inside the NLE. Magic Bullet Looks provides a library of cinematic LUT-based grades; Denoiser III removes high-ISO grain; Colorista delivers node-based secondary grading in-timeline.

Sapphire Suite by Boris FX

Over 270 GPU-accelerated effects and transitions for Premiere and After Effects. Sapphire's lens flares, glows, and film-damage effects are the industry benchmark — used in virtually every major broadcast and streaming production.

Trapcode Suite

Red Giant's flagship suite powering Particular (3D particle systems), Form (mesh-based particles), Mir (3D terrain and wireframes), and Tao (3D geometry along paths). Indispensable for large-scale organic motion graphics.

X-Particles

Cinema 4D's most advanced particle and VFX simulation system. Handles fluid dynamics, granular simulations, crowd systems, and multi-physics interactions — all within the C4D environment and fully renderable via Redshift and Octane.

Optical Flares

Video Copilot's physics-based lens flare plugin with a real-time 3D preview. Used to add optically accurate camera lens artifacts, anamorphic streaks, and chromatic glow to composites — bridging the gap between CGI and live-action shots.

Technical Philosophy

Speed Without Sacrifice

Every tool in this stack is chosen with a single objective: to eliminate the gap between an idea and its execution. Speed matters — but not at the cost of craft. By building deep fluency across the entire pipeline, from concept sketch in Figma to final color grade in DaVinci Resolve, I can make real-time creative decisions at every stage rather than being forced to wait for renders, exports, or specialist hand-offs. The result is a faster iteration cycle with zero reduction in visual quality.

True technical mastery is invisible. The audience should never see the complexity behind the frame — they should only feel the impact of what appears on screen. This philosophy drives every hardware purchase, every plugin investment, and every hour spent learning the internals of a new tool. When the technology disappears into muscle memory, creativity becomes limitless.
