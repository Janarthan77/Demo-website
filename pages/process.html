The 3-Step Pipeline

How we move from initial contact to final delivery.

01

Discovery & Quote

You submit an inquiry. We hop on a 15-minute call to align on creative vision, deadlines, and budget. I send over a formal SOW and quote.

02

Asset Handoff & Edit

You provide footage, brand guidelines, and assets via Frame.io or Drive. I begin the offline edit and motion graphics blockout.

03

Review & Final Delivery

You review V1 via Frame.io. We iterate through the agreed revision rounds. Final masters are delivered in requested codecs (ProRes, H.264).

Clear Revision Policies

Revisions are a natural part of the creative process. To prevent scope creep, all quotes explicitly define the number of revision rounds included (typically 2 to 3).

Additional rounds or total creative pivots requested after V1 are billed at my standard hourly rate of $150/hr. I use Frame.io for all review links, allowing you to leave precise, frame-accurate notes to ensure nothing gets lost in translation.
