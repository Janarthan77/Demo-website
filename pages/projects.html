Selected Projects

A deep dive into three of my most successful recent campaigns, breaking down the specific roles and challenges overcome.

Full 3D Pipeline

VR Headset Launch

Tasked with generating a massive 60-second CGI teaser for a next-generation VR headset. Modeled the entire headset in Cinema 4D based on CAD files, built a complex lighting rig to highlight the glass and matte plastics, and animated dynamic camera sweeps.

  • Role: Lead 3D Artist & Motion Designer
  • Timeline: 4 Weeks
  • Outcome: 2.5M Views across social within 24 hours.
VFX Compositing

Neon Genesis Web Series

Lead compositing artist on a 6-part indie sci-fi web series. Handled over 150 complex VFX shots including wire-removals, extensive rotoscoping, holographic screen replacements, and fully CGI set extensions of a dystopian city skyline.

  • Role: Compositing Supervisor
  • Timeline: 8 Weeks
  • Outcome: Nominated for Best VFX at IndieWebFest.
Motion Graphics

Spotify Wrapped Campaign

Designed a hyper-energetic, rhythm-driven motion graphics piece for a digital marketing push. Relied heavily on kinetic typography, rapid camera transitions, and explosive color palettes synchronized perfectly to a custom music track.

  • Role: Motion Graphics Animator
  • Timeline: 2 Weeks
  • Outcome: 15% higher click-through-rate than previous campaigns.
